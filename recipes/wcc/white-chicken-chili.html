Delicious White Chicken Chili Recipe

Description

This white chicken chili is an incredibly delicious and healthy recipe!

You'll be wanting to make this weekly, and it's perfect for meal prep!

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • White onion
  • Spices
  • Chicken broth or stock

Steps

  1. Add chicken to botoom of instant pot, along with spices, diced onion, and chicken broth
  2. Cook for 30 minutes
  3. Pull chicken out, shred it, then put it back in pot
  4. Stir and enjoy!
