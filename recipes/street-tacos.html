Authentic Street Tacos Recipe

Descripton

These authentic Mexican street tacos make for a great date dish!

Or double the batch size for a dinner party! Everyone loves these tacos!

Ingredients

  • Flank or skirt steak
  • Corn tortillas
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Mix steak and spices in a bowl
  2. Let sit for 30 minutes
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan on medium-high heat, then add steak
  4. Cook steak while stirring occasionally for 8 minutes
  5. Build tacos with corn tortillas and any desired toppings, and enjoy!
