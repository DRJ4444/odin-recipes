Ooey Gooey Chocolate Cake Recipe

Descripton

This Ooey Gooey Chocolate Cake recipe will be sure to impress at your next dinner party!

With a couple secret ingredients, your guests will be amazed at how yummy this cake is. It's up to you if you want to share your secrets or not!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cocoa
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Mix eggs and cocoa
  2. Gradually mix in flour
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
  4. Let rest for 10 minutes, then enjoy!
